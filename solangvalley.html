Sollang Valley

Images

Description

This tourist attraction, known as Solang Valley, perched between Solang Village and Beas Kund, is a famous hill spot widely renowned among holidaymakers for its snow-capped mountains and picturesque glaciers that are a sight to behold. Also referred to as Snow Valley, Solang Valley promises to offer a delightful holidaying experience to all its visitors and has a pristine, snow-white magnificence that catches the attention of people from different corners of the world. The unmissable landscapes are hard to miss here and the unparalleled beauty, when combined with a magnificent charm, makes you fall in love with this place all over again.
